Egg and Toast Breakfast

Thank you for choosing our recipe, you wont be sorry this eggs on toast will be the best you've ever had.

Let's start with what we need"

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper

Next let's make it

  1. Toast the bread
  2. Cook the eggs in a pan with butter or oil
  3. Put egg on toast, add salt and pepper
  4. Enjoy!
