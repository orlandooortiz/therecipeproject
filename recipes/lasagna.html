lasagna

This wonderful lasagna is not all that hard to make. buckle up and we'll get started on making this wonderful dish

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 1LB ground beef(or ground turkey)
  • 1 Jar (24oz) pasta sauce (marinara or meat sauce)
  • 1 container (15oz) Ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 2-3 cups shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Optional Garlic powder or Italian seasoning for more authentic flavor

How to make the lasagna:

  1. Cook the Noodles

    Boil lasagna noodles in salted water according to the package. Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the meat

    In a pan, cook the fround beef over medium heat until browned. Season lightly with salt, pepper, and optional Garlic/italian seasoning

  3. Add sauce

    Pour the pasta sauce into the meat. Stir and let it simmer for about 5 minutes.

  4. Mix the Ricotta

    In a bowl, mix ricotta chees with 1 Egg and a little bit of salt and pepper

  5. Preaheat oven

    Set oven to 375 degrees F

  6. Layer the lasagna

    In a baking dish:

    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom
    • Add noodles
    • Spread ricotta mixture
    • Sprinkle Mozzarella
    • Repeat layers until ingreadients are used
    • Finish with sauce on top and sprinkle with mozzarella and parmesan
  7. Bake

    Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes

  8. Uncover and Finish

    Remove foil and bake another 10-15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.

  9. Rest and Serve

    Let it sit for 10 minutes before cutting(this helps it hold together).
