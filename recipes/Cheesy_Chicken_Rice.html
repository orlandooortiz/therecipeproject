Cheesy Chicken Rice

This Cheesy Chicken Rice will knock your socks off, so let's dive in

Let's start with what we need:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 can coocked chicken(or rotisserie chicken)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Now how to make it!

  1. Cook the rice in the water(follow instruction on rice bag)
  2. Stir in chicken once rice is done and cooked through and through
  3. Add cheese, salt, and pepper, then mix until cheese is melted

And just like that you've made Cheesy Chicken Rice!
